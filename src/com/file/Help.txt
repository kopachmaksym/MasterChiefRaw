This program includes preparation of food salad.
Some types of food are already added to the list for simplification.
There are five different options in the menu - com.food.Food, Salad, Read list of salats from file, Help and Exit.
When you enter the number '1', you go to a submenu where you can perform various operations with food.
When you enter the number '2', you go to the submenu, where you can perform various operations with the salad.
When you enter the number '3', you get a list of created before salats.
When you enter the number '4', you get a help that can help you understand the program.
When you enter the number '5', you end the program.
